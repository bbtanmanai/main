## 1. 대표이미지 `c{N}.png`

```
Create a single character illustration in 9:16 portrait ratio (1080×1920px).

=== CANVAS ===
- Final image ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920 pixels)
- Solid uniform flat background: #FFFF00 (pure yellow)
- NO gradients, NO textures, NO patterns in the background

=== CHARACTER FRAMING ===
- Upper body visible from head to just above the knees (approx. knee-level crop)
- Character centered horizontally
- Top of head has at least 5% margin from top edge
- Bottom edge cuts at approximately mid-thigh to knee level (NOT full body, NOT feet)
- Body/torso faces forward in a natural, relaxed posture
- NO arms on the body or costume — the arm area (shoulder socket) must be cleanly closed/rounded

=== BLACK OUTLINE STANDARD ===
- MANDATORY: Apply a clean BLACK OUTLINE around the entire character silhouette
  • Outline width: 4~6px at 1080×1920 scale
  • Outline color: pure black (#000000)
  • Outline must be smooth, consistent, and follow the character silhouette precisely
  • Every part of the character (hair, clothes) must have this black outline
  • This outline enables clean background removal and is a LinkDrop brand standard

=== ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS ===
- NO white outline (only black outline is allowed)
- NO transparent or white background (must be #FFFF00)
- NO text, labels, watermarks
- NO full body / feet visible (upper body only, cropped at knee level)
- NO arms on the body — arms are provided as separate arm_Grid overlay images
- Arms must NOT appear attached to the torso; shoulder area ends cleanly without arm stubs
```

## 2. mouth_Grid `c{N}_mouth_Grid.png`

```
Create a single 16:9 reference sheet image containing exactly 3 panels of the uploaded character's mouth area, arranged in a horizontal row.

=== CANVAS ===
- Final image ratio: 16:9 (e.g., 1920×1080 pixels)
- Solid uniform flat background: #FFFF00 (pure yellow)
- NO gradients, NO textures, NO patterns in the background
- NO borders, NO dividing lines, NO gaps, NO frames between panels

=== PANEL LAYOUT ===
- Exactly 3 panels. NOT 2, NOT 4. Exactly THREE.
- Each panel occupies exactly 1/3 of the total canvas width
- All 3 panels share the EXACT same:
  • Zoom level (identical magnification)
  • Vertical crop position (identical Y-axis framing)
  • Horizontal centering (face centered in each panel)
  • Lighting and color grading

=== CROP / FRAMING ===
- Camera frames from JUST BELOW THE EYES down to the CHIN
- Visible areas: lower cheeks, nose tip, nasolabial folds, mouth, chin, jawline
- The very top of the frame may show the bottom edge of the eyes (acceptable)
- DO NOT show forehead, hair, or ears
- DO NOT crop too tight (chin must be fully visible with some margin below)

=== THE 3 MOUTH STATES ===
Panel 1 (LEFT):   CLOSED — Mouth gently closed, lips together, natural resting expression.
                   No tension in the jaw. Relaxed, neutral.
Panel 2 (CENTER): HALF OPEN — Mouth slightly parted, as if mid-word during speech.
                   Upper and lower teeth may be barely visible. Lips relaxed but separated.
Panel 3 (RIGHT):  FULLY OPEN — Mouth clearly open wide, as if pronouncing "아" or "오".
                   Upper teeth, lower teeth, and tongue clearly visible. Active speaking pose.

=== BLACK OUTLINE STANDARD ===
- MANDATORY: Apply a clean BLACK OUTLINE around the character/part silhouette
  • Outline width: 4~6px at 1920×1080 scale
  • Outline color: pure black (#000000)
  • Outline must be smooth, consistent, and follow the character silhouette precisely
  • This outline enables clean background removal (yellow background chroma key)
- You MUST exactly replicate the art style, line quality, color palette, and rendering technique of the uploaded reference image
- DO NOT switch styles from the reference image

=== ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS ===
- NO white outline (only black outline is allowed)
- NO gray or white background (must be #FFFF00)
- NO text, labels, numbers, or watermarks anywhere
- NO panel borders, frames, or dividing lines
- NO extra panels (must be exactly 3)
- NO full face (only lower face area)
- NO style change from the reference image
```

## 5. arm_Grid `c{N}_arm_Grid.png`

```
Create a single 16:9 reference sheet image containing exactly 4 panels of the uploaded character's RIGHT ARM in different poses, arranged in a horizontal row.

=== CANVAS ===
- Final image ratio: 16:9 (e.g., 1920×1080 pixels)
- Solid uniform flat background: #FFFF00 (pure yellow)
- NO gradients, NO patterns

=== PANEL LAYOUT ===
- Exactly 4 panels. NOT 3, NOT 5. Exactly FOUR.
- Each panel occupies exactly 1/4 of the total canvas width
- All 4 panels share the EXACT same shoulder anchor position
  (the shoulder joint should be at the same X,Y position in every panel)

=== CROP / FRAMING ===
- Show ONLY the right arm: from the shoulder joint down to the fingertips
- The shoulder connection point (the top of the arm where it originates) must be visible
- IMPORTANT: The reference character's BODY/COSTUME has NO ARM attached — the arm starts
  directly at the shoulder socket. Match this exact anatomy: arm begins cleanly at the shoulder cap.
- Everything else (head, body, left arm, legs) must be COMPLETELY HIDDEN by yellow background
- Hand and fingers must be fully visible, not cropped

=== THE 4 ARM POSES ===
Panel 1 (FAR LEFT):    DOWN — Arm hanging naturally at the side, completely relaxed.
                        Hand open, fingers naturally curled. Gravity pulling downward.
Panel 2 (CENTER-LEFT):  HALF RAISED — Arm raised to ~45° angle, elbow bent.
                        Forearm pointing slightly forward/upward. Natural mid-gesture.
Panel 3 (CENTER-RIGHT): WAVE — Arm raised high above shoulder level, hand open with
                         fingers spread, classic friendly waving/greeting gesture. Energetic.
Panel 4 (FAR RIGHT):    POINT — Arm extended forward horizontally, index finger extended
                         and pointing toward the viewer/camera. Other fingers curled.
                         Explaining/emphasizing gesture.

=== BLACK OUTLINE STANDARD ===
- MANDATORY: Apply a clean BLACK OUTLINE around the arm/hand silhouette
  • Outline width: 4~6px at 1920×1080 scale
  • Outline color: pure black (#000000)
  • Outline must be smooth, consistent, and follow the arm silhouette precisely
  • This outline enables clean background removal (yellow background chroma key)
- The arm must wear the EXACT same clothing as the reference image
- Skin tone and hand proportions must match the reference character
- Line quality and rendering style must be identical to reference
- DO NOT switch rendering styles

=== ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS ===
- NO white outline (only black outline is allowed)
- NO gray or white or transparent background (must be #FFFF00)
- NO body, head, or other limbs visible (arm ONLY, rest covered by yellow background)
- NO text, labels, numbers, or watermarks
- NO panel borders or dividing lines
- NO style change from reference
- The shoulder anchor point must be CONSISTENT across all 4 panels
```
